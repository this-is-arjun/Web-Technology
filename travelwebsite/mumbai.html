Mumbai- sapno ki nagri

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a vibrant and fast-paced city located on the west coast along the Arabian Sea. Known as the “City of Dreams,” it is home to Bollywood, major industries, and diverse cultures. From the iconic Gateway of India and Marine Drive to its bustling local trains and street food, Mumbai blends modern energy with rich history and tradition.
